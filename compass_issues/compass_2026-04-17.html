MIL Fade

SEA Fade
FADE: Seattle's bullpen looks worse than it should right now, giving up runs at a much higher rate than their strikeout numbers would suggest. The gap between how they are actually pitching and how many runs they are allowing is a red flag. Fade the Mariners when the game goes to their relief corps.
LAA Fade
FADE: The Angels bullpen ranks 28th in the league over the last week with a 6.79 ERA, and this has been a problem for a while now. Unlike some other teams, their struggles look pretty real across the board. Avoid Los Angeles when the starters hand it over to the pen.
ATL Value
VALUE: Atlanta's starting pitchers have been dealing lately, putting up a 2.27 ERA over the last week and ranking 6th in the majors. They are keeping hitters off base consistently with a solid WHIP of 1.23. Back the Braves when one of their starters is on the mound.
MIL Value
VALUE: Milwaukee's starters have been sharp this week, ranking 5th in the league with a 2.36 ERA and a very stingy 0.845 WHIP. They are barely letting anyone on base, which is a great sign heading into the weekend. Back Milwaukee in games started by one of their starters.
ATH Value
VALUE: The Athletics rotation has been showing some positive momentum lately, winning more games than expected recently. Their starters are performing right in line with how they should be, so there are no smoke-and-mirrors concerns here. Worth backing Oakland when their starters take the ball.
CIN Value
VALUE: Cincinnati's starters have been holding their own even while facing a tough schedule lately. Their numbers are consistent and there is nothing misleading about how they have been pitching. Back the Reds rotation even against tougher opponents.
ARI Value
VALUE: Arizona's offense has been one of the better ones in baseball lately, ranking 6th this week and averaging over 5 runs a game. Their lineup has been hitting well above average compared to the rest of the league. Back the Diamondbacks when they have a favorable pitching matchup.
BOS Value
VALUE: Boston's lineup has been producing runs at a solid clip, averaging over 5 runs per game and ranking 7th in the league this week. That is especially impressive given they have been facing a tough schedule. Back the Red Sox offense to keep delivering even in hard matchups.

Methodology
Rolling windows — 7, 14, and 28-day stats from completed games only. Postponed games excluded.
Momentum — rank_28d - rank_7d. Positive = outperforming recent baseline. Negative = declining.
Schedule strength — Value on Hard schedule is stronger.
ERA-FIP gap — ERA minus FIP. Gap over 1.0 = ERA likely to regress down (unlucky). Under -1.0 = may regress up.
Signals — Value = momentum +5 or more on Avg/Hard. Fade = -5 or worse on Easy/Avg.
Straight rolling ERA with no leverage weighting.
